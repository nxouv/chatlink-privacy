Privacy Policy

ChatLink - Open Channel from YouTube Live Chat

Overview

ChatLink is a Chrome extension that allows you to open a YouTube channel by clicking on a commenter's avatar in YouTube Live chat. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

ChatLink does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

Permissions Used

  • storage - Used only to save your ON/OFF preference locally
  • scripting - Used to apply settings to YouTube pages
  • Host permission (youtube.com) - Required to add click functionality to avatars in YouTube Live chat

Third-Party Services

ChatLink does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date below.
